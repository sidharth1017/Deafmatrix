DeafMatrix

Our Mission

DeafMatrix is all about bringing together deaf community on one platform where they can connect people with similar interests, share their experiences, and provide latest news, trends, and events around the world. There are 466 million deaf people in the world that has some kind of disabling hearing loss. DeafMatrix is a social networking app for the Deaf community. This app will be of service to millions of deaf friends around the world to build new connections with the help of today's most advanced technology.

FAQ

How much does DeafMatrix cost?

It is absolutely free. No hidden fees.

Who can access this app?

Anyone including non-deaf person can access this, but in order to create an account, you must be at least 18 or older.

Does this app require you to verify your account?

It is not required however verifying your account will let people know that you are a real person. The check button on your profile will determine that you are verified deaf.

How do you verify your account?

You can take a picture of your face with your assistive device shown and sign (I love you), but if you do not wear an assistive device you can upload your audiogram. All photos or audiograms will be reviewed by an actual DeafMatrix team member.

How can I report someone?

If you feel like someone has an inappropriate post on the News Feed, you can go to the post and tap three dots on the top right side and can report it. Our team will review it and take necessary action against it.

Do I need to send a friend request before I can message?

You are not required to send a friend request, however if you feel like you don’t want to be friends with that person you have an option to always block that person.

I created an account with Facebook and I deactivated it. Can I still use DeafMatrix?

No. DeafMatrix will not be able to find your account because it is linked with Facebook. However you have the option to create an account with your phone number but your data will be lost.
